Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Breeze Safaris is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process and safeguard your information when you visit https://breezesafaris.com or contact us regarding our services.

1. Who We Are

Our website address is: https://breezesafaris.com.

2. Data Controller

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation, GDPR, Breeze Safaris acts as the Data Controller in relation to personal data collected through this website.

Brand name: Breeze Safaris
Website: https://breezesafaris.com
Contact email: info@breezesafaris.com

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us using the email above.

3. Our Business Model

Breeze Safaris is not a licensed tour operator. We act as an intermediary between clients and licensed local tour operators in Tanzania.

We design tailor made safari itineraries and connect clients with carefully selected local partners who execute the travel services. Once a booking is confirmed, certain personal data may be shared with the relevant local operator strictly for the purpose of delivering the travel services.

4. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Telephone number
  • Country of residence
  • Travel preferences and itinerary requirements
  • Passport information when required for bookings
  • Any additional information voluntarily provided through contact forms or email communication

We may also collect technical data automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Pages visited
  • Date and time of access

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Your consent when submitting a contact form
  • Pre contractual steps taken at your request
  • Performance of a contract once a booking is confirmed
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests in operating and improving our services

6. Purpose of Processing

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Responding to enquiries
  • Preparing customised safari proposals
  • Coordinating bookings with local tour operators
  • Communicating important travel information
  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Marketing activities where consent has been provided

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Your personal data may be shared with:

  • Licensed local tour operators in Tanzania responsible for delivering the travel services
  • Accommodation providers and service suppliers relevant to your itinerary
  • Website hosting providers
  • Email service providers
  • Marketing and analytics providers such as Google Analytics, Google Ads and Meta Ads
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law

Local tour operators receiving your data may act as independent Data Controllers under applicable laws in their jurisdiction.

We ensure that data is shared only when necessary and proportionate to deliver the requested services.

8. International Data Transfers

As we work with partners based in Tanzania, your personal data may be transferred outside the European Economic Area.

When such transfers occur, we take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with GDPR requirements.

By requesting our services, you acknowledge that such international transfers may be necessary for the performance of your travel arrangements.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfil the purposes outlined in this Policy
  • Comply with legal, tax and accounting obligations
  • Resolve disputes

When data is no longer required, it will be securely deleted or anonymised.

10. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@breezesafaris.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Analyse website traffic
  • Improve user experience
  • Deliver relevant advertising

You may control or disable cookies through your browser settings. A cookie consent banner may appear when you first visit our website.

12. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.

We encourage users to review this Policy periodically.

14. Exercising Your Data Rights

To exercise any of your rights under applicable data protection laws, including access, rectification, restriction, objection or erasure of your personal data, please contact:

info@breezesafaris.com

We may request proof of identity to ensure data security. All valid requests will be handled within the timeframes required under GDPR.
