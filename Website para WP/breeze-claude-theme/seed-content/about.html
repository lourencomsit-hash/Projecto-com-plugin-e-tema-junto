About Us

We are two safari specialists living in Tanzania, creating tailor-made journeys with trusted local partners and private guiding.

Our approach is simple: understand your travel style, build the right route for the season, and keep every part of your experience smooth from first call to final game drive.

From classic northern circuit safaris to bush-and-beach journeys with Zanzibar, we design each itinerary with local knowledge, practical logistics and personal support.
