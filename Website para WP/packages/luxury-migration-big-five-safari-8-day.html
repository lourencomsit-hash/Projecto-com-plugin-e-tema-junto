Itinerary

Day 1 • Arrival In Arusha

Airport pickup, warm welcome and private transfer to your hotel for rest before safari departure.

Day 2 • Tarangire National Park

Game drive among baobabs and elephant herds, then continue to your luxury lodge.

Stay: Elewana Tarangire Treetops

Day 3 • Drive To Northern Serengeti

Long scenic transfer with wildlife en route as you move into the migration zone.

Stay: Kubu Kubu Tented Lodge

Day 4 • Northern Serengeti Migration Focus

Full safari day searching for river crossing activity, large herds and predator action.

Stay: Kubu Kubu Tented Lodge

Day 5 • Northern To Central Serengeti

Game drive transfer into central plains, one of the strongest areas for big cat encounters.

Stay: Kubu Kubu Tented Lodge

Day 6 • Serengeti To Ngorongoro Highlands

Morning safari and transfer to the crater rim area for a relaxed evening.

Stay: Ngorongoro Lion's Paw Lodge

Day 7 • Ngorongoro Crater & Lake Manyara

Descend into Ngorongoro for a high-density game drive, then transfer to Lake Manyara area.

Stay: Lake Manyara Kilimamoja Lodge

Day 8 • Lake Manyara Safari & Departure

Final game drive through forest and lake landscapes, then transfer for departure.
