Tanzania & Zanzibar: 9-Day Honeymoon Journey

Private tour for 2 travelers with a 7-seat safari vehicle, immersive Tarangire game drives, a scenic Manyara stay and a beach finale in Zanzibar.

Starting from $3,580 per person

Inquire

Trip Details

  • Duration: 9 Days
  • Travel style: Private journey for 2 travelers (special occasion / honeymoon)
  • Safari transport: 7-seat safari vehicle with pop-up roof
  • Start/End: Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO)

Itinerary

Day 1 • Arrival In Tanzania

Arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport and private transfer to Arusha.

Stay: Four Points by Sheraton (Arusha) · Dinner included

Day 2 • Tarangire National Park

Drive to Tarangire for a full-day game drive focused on wildlife concentrations near the Tarangire River.

Stay: Lake Burunge Baobab · Full board

Day 3 • Tarangire Exploration

Full safari day across central riverine habitats and quieter wildlife sectors.

Stay: Lake Burunge Baobab · Full board

Day 4 • Tarangire Deep Safari

Another full day with highlights including elephant herds, lions and leopards, plus southern woodland and marsh zones.

Stay: Lake Burunge Baobab · Full board

Day 5 • Manyara Secret Lodge

Transfer to Manyara Secret Lodge. Free afternoon to relax or join an optional Maasai village cultural visit.

Stay: Manyara Secret Lodge · Full board

Day 6 • Flight To Zanzibar

Transfer back to Arusha for lunch and domestic flight to Zanzibar, followed by private transfer to your beach resort.

Stay: Next Paradise Boutique Resort · Dinner included

Day 7 • Zanzibar Beach Time

Relax on Pwani Mchangani beach and enjoy the oceanfront setting at your own pace.

Stay: Next Paradise Boutique Resort · Half board

Day 8 • Zanzibar Leisure Day

Free beach day with time for swimming, rest and optional island activities.

Stay: Next Paradise Boutique Resort · Half board

Day 9 • Departure

Breakfast and transfer to Zanzibar Airport for local connection flight to JRO for your international departure.

  • Internal flights: Arusha to Zanzibar and Zanzibar to JRO
  • Accommodation as per itinerary
