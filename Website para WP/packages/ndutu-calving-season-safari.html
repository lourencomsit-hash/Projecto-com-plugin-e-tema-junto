Itinerary

Day 1 • Arrival In Arusha

Airport meet-and-greet, private transfer and relaxed check-in before safari start.

Stay: Arusha Planet Lodge

Day 2 • Into Ndutu Migration Zone

Travel to Ndutu and begin game viewing in one of Tanzania's top seasonal wildlife regions.

Stay: Ang'ata Migration Bologonja Camp

Day 3 • Full Day Ndutu Safari

Morning to evening game drives focused on migration herds and predator activity.

Stay: Ang'ata Migration Bologonja Camp

Day 4 • Ndutu Wildlife Day

Another full safari day in Ndutu for deeper tracking and varied sightings across the ecosystem.

Stay: Ang'ata Migration Bologonja Camp

Day 5 • Ngorongoro Crater Safari

Descend into the crater for a concentrated wildlife day in one of Tanzania's best ecosystems.

Stay: Embalakai Ngorongoro Camp

Day 6 • Return To Arusha

Scenic drive back to Arusha with drop-off at your hotel or airport.
